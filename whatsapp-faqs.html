1. How to download WhatsApp?
Go to Play Store (Android) or App Store (iPhone), search for "WhatsApp", and tap Install.
2. How to create a WhatsApp account?
Open WhatsApp, enter your phone number, verify it via SMS, then set your profile name and picture.
3. How to send a message?
Open a chat, type your message in the text box, and tap the send button (üì§).
4. How to make a voice call?
Open a chat and tap the phone icon (üìû) at the top right corner.
5. How to make a video call?
Open a chat and tap the camera icon (üìπ) at the top right corner.
6. How to send a photo or video?
Tap the attachment (üìé) icon, select Gallery or Camera, choose your media, and send it.
7. How to create a WhatsApp group?
Tap New Chat > New Group, select contacts, set group name, and tap Create.
8. How to leave a group?
Open the group > tap on group name > scroll and tap 'Exit Group'.
9. How to delete a message?
Long-press the message > tap trash icon > choose 'Delete for me' or 'Delete for everyone'.
10. How to change profile picture?
Go to Settings > Tap your profile > Tap the photo > Choose a new image.
11. How to check if someone read your message?
Look for blue check marks under the message, or long-press the message > tap 'Info'.
12. How to block someone?
Open the chat > tap three dots > More > Block.
13. How to enable WhatsApp Web?
Go to web.whatsapp.com > Open WhatsApp on your phone > tap Menu > WhatsApp Web > Scan QR Code.
14. How to update WhatsApp?
Go to Play Store/App Store > Search WhatsApp > Tap Update (if available).
15. How to change the wallpaper in chats?
Open WhatsApp > Settings > Chats > Wallpaper > Choose wallpaper and set it.
