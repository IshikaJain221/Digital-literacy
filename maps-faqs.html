üó∫ Google Maps - Frequently Asked Questions

1. How to install Google Maps?
Go to Play Store/App Store > Search Google Maps > Tap Install.
2. How to search a location?
Open app > Type place name in search bar > Tap result.
3. How to start navigation?
Search destination > Tap 'Directions' > Start.
4. How to change transportation mode?
On directions screen, choose Car, Bus, Walk, or Bike icons.
5. How to share location?
Tap blue dot > Share location > Choose contact or app.
6. How to download offline maps?
Tap your profile > Offline maps > Select area > Download.
7. How to check traffic?
Enable Traffic Layer from the layers menu in top-right corner.
8. How to change map type?
Use Layers icon > Select Satellite, Terrain, etc.
9. How to save favorite places?
Search a place > Tap 'Save' > Choose a list like Favorites.
10. How to switch to Street View?
Drag yellow person icon to the map location to enter Street View.
