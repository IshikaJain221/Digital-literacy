üí∞ Paytm - Frequently Asked Questions

1. How to install Paytm?
Open Play Store or App Store, search Paytm, and click Install.
2. How to create a Paytm account?
Open Paytm > Enter your mobile number > Verify OTP > Set password.
3. How to add money to wallet?
Go to Wallet > Tap 'Add Money' > Enter amount > Choose payment method.
4. How to recharge a mobile?
Tap 'Recharge' > Enter number > Select operator > Pay using wallet or UPI.
5. How to pay at a shop using Paytm?
Scan the shopkeeper's QR code using Paytm Scanner > Enter amount > Pay.
6. How to transfer money to a bank account?
Go to 'To Bank A/c' > Enter account details > Enter amount > Confirm transfer.
7. How to book train tickets?
Select Train Tickets > Enter details > Choose train > Make payment.
8. How to check wallet balance?
Go to Wallet > Your balance is shown on top.
9. How to pay electricity bill?
Tap 'Electricity' > Select board > Enter customer number > View bill > Pay.
10. How to view past transactions?
Go to Passbook > Choose account > View transaction history.
